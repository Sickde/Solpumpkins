COMMUNITY BENEFITS

To be eligible for each airdrop, you need to keep your solpumpking NFTs in the same wallet used during the minting process.

There are only 666 airdrops so only the first ones to fulfill the requirements will get the airdrop while stock last.

Diamond hands airdrop tier 1

Own 10 solpumpkins and keep them for 60 days counting from the minting launch date. You will get 5 solpumpkins.

Diamond hands airdrop tier 2

Own 5 solpumpkins and keep them for 30 days counting from the minting launch date. You will get 3 solpumpkins.

Diamond hands airdrop tier 3

Own 2 solpumpkins and keep them for 15 days counting from the minting launch date. You will get 2 solpumpkins.
